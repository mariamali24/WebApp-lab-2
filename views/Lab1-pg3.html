Chicken Penne Pasta

Type of Cusine: Italian
Type of Meal: Dinner
Rating 3
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts, sliced
salt, to taste
pepper, to taste
2 teaspoons italian seasoning
1 teaspoon paprika
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 cups spinach
4 small tomatoes, diced
1 ½ cups cream
1 cup shredded parmesan cheese, plus more for serving
½ teaspoon red pepper flakes, plus more for garnish
10 oz penne pasta, cooked until al dente
3 tablespoons Chopped fresh parsley, for garnish
Preparation:
1. In a deep skillet, add the chicken and season with salt, pepper, Italian seasoning, and paprika. Cook until no longer pink inside.
2. Add the garlic and cook until softened.
3. Add spinach and tomatoes and cook until the spinach is wilted.
4. Add the cream, Parmesan, an red pepper flakes and bring to a boil.
5. Add the penne and bacon, and stir until fully coated in sauce.
6. Serve with parsley, Parmesan, and red pepper flakes, if desired.
